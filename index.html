📖 The Story

In 2026, the Estonian island of Saaremaa — population 31,000 — declared independence and renamed itself the Republic of Varde. Small, isolated, and starting from scratch.

But Varde's founders had a radical idea: instead of inventing policies from scratch, they would simply copy the best policy from every country on Earth — Finland's UBI, Taiwan's healthcare, Singapore's housing, Estonia's e-government, Norway's wealth fund, Portugal's drug laws.

This simulation asks: what happens if you actually do that? Watch Varde grow year by year as 20 world-best policies come online — and see where it ends up compared to real nations.
